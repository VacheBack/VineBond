Grosses Gewächs (GG)

Germany's highest classification for dry wines, equivalent to Grand Cru. Awarded only to vineyards with exceptional terroir and producers holding VDP membership.

Erstes Gewächs (1G)

The Rheingau-specific "First Growth" classification, predating the national GG system. Covers historic Cistercian and estate vineyards with documented provenance since the 12th century.

4 1G Sites

Rheingau Terroir

South-facing slopes above the Rhine between Rüdesheim and Hochheim. Phyllite slate, quartzite, loess, and limestone create the diversity behind Germany's most celebrated Rieslings.
