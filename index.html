Physical Routing · On-Site Access

Scan a Vineyard QR Code to Unlock Exclusive Content

Each VineBond partner winery places physical QR codes throughout their estate — at the cellar door, tasting room, and vineyard rows. A single scan connects you instantly to that winery's profile, tasting notes, and bookable experiences.

1
Spot the VineBond marker Look for the burgundy QR placard at the vineyard entrance, cellar, or tasting room.
2
Open your camera app No extra app needed — iOS and Android camera apps scan directly. Just point and hold.
3
Access the winery instantly VineBond loads the winery's live profile: current tastings, pricing, reviews, and booking.
4
Earn VineClub points Every scan at a partner winery credits your VineClub account with loyalty points automatically.
